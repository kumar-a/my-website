Abhishek Kumar

Senior Research Fellow

ICAR–NBPGR, New Delhi

I am working as a senior research fellow in the National Mission for Sustaining Himalayan Ecosystem (NMSHE) project at ICAR–National Bureau of Plant Genetic Resources, New Delhi. My research broadly focuses to understand the ecosystem structure and functions in the context of global environmental change. Guided by the values of collaboration, integrity, and open science, I aim to contribute meaningfully to ecological research and inform evidence-based management strategies.
